On The Day Weekend

So that we can celebrate for as long as possible, we're taking over an entire Bank Holiday! After the wedding on the Saturday, we're organising a BBQ on Bournemouth beach for the Sunday and would love to see you all there! Then you have Monday to recover...

Getting To (And From!) The Larmer Tree

The Larmer Tree Gardens are 11 acres of beautiful surroundings on the Dorset and Wiltshire border. It's the perfect rural location for us to celebrate our wedding with friends and family.

With that in mind, get those babysitters arranged and have another drink as we will be providing a coach to get people home safely, stopping in Wimborne, Poole and Bournemouth.

Please confirm your interest by e-mail so we can secure the booking as soon as possible.

Accommodation

If you are planning to stay for the weekend, we recommend finding a hotel in Bournemouth and travelling to the Larmer Tree on the day, then taking the coach back.

This will mean you are centrally located and close to us on the Sunday for the beach BBQ (details to follow!). Get in touch if you want advice on where to stay in Bournemouth.
