INFORMATION

The Venue

The Larmer Tree Gardens are 11 acres of ornate garden and beautiful surroundings so come prepared! You'll have plenty of time to explore the grounds, see the pnds and peacocks and bask in some early summer sun! Feel free to bring rugs, sun-cream, panamas, umbrellas, wellie boots!

Dress Code

Summer Garden Party

Most of the day will be outside on the Lawn so dress appropriately! Heels are acceptable for the Ladies, but feel free to bring a change of shoes or we'll have some flip flops and heel stoppers for you also!

Wedding Breakfast

We'll be serving a delicious feast to satisfy all tastes, but please let us know if you have any special dietary requirements.

Getting To (And From!) The Larmer Tree

Driving

The postcode for the Gardens is SP5 5PY, however most sat nav systems will send you to Rushmore Golf Course, so be careful! The main approach roads are the A354 and A30, and from them you can follow the Brown Flower heritage signs. For more details, check the Larmer Tree website.

Taxis

The Gardens are on the Dorest/Wiltshire border, in the middle of nowhere! Please pre-book your tax before the day, as it will not be possible to hail a cab in the evening.

If you are looking for someone to share a cab with, or if you have space available then please let us know and we'll try to put people together.

Parking

There is a car park right next to the Gardens and free of charge. You are welcome to leave cars over night, but this is at your own risk and the Larmer Tree is not responsible for your cars or belongings.

Where To Stay

The Larmer Tree is really in the middle of nowhere, though there are a couple of hotels nearby, and also some towns not too far away - Shaftesbury, Blandford and Wimborne are all close and have a selection of places to stay - but dont forget to book a cab!

However, don't forget that it is a Bank Holiday weekend, and so if you want to make a weekend of it, and join us for the wind down BBQ on the Sunday, then you might consider staying in Bournemouth so you will be centrally located. Again, plenty of choice there, but book up quick and don't forget the cab! (Please let us know your arrangements and we can try to help you with everything.)

You can also camp at the Larmer Tree, and stay in your campervan if you have one, but please tell us in advance if you want to do this.

Google, AirBNB, Expedia, Booking.com, etc will all do a far better job than we can of recommending hotels, but please if you feel a bit lost of want a bit of advice on what to do - just get in touch!

The BBQ

Yep, one day is just not enough so we'll be continuing on the Sunday with a relaxing afternoon on the Beach. Just bring a burger, sausage, and a bun and join us.

We'll aim for the Brankome part of the Beach starting from about 14:00, so head that way and join us!
