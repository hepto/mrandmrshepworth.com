The Larmer Tree Gardens

The Larmer Tree Gardens are 11 acres of ornate garden and beautiful surroundings so come prepared! Feel free to bring rugs, sun-cream, panamas, umbrellas, wellie boots; ladies bring a change of shoes as we will be on grass and gravle - we reccomend flip flops or wedges. Heels are acceptable for the lawns, if tyou do not mind the risk of sinking!

Getting To (And From!) The Larmer Tree

Driving

The postcode for the Gardens is SP5 5PY, however most sat nav systems will send you to Rushmore Golf Course, so be careful! The main approach roads are the A354 and A30, and from them you can follow the Brown Flower heritage signs. For more details, check the Larmer Tree website.

Taxis

The Gardens are on the Dorest/Wiltshire border, in the middle of nowhere! Please pre-book your tax before the day, as it will not be possible to hail a cab in the evening.

If you are looking for someone to share a cab with, or if you have space available then please let us know and we'll try to put people together.

Parking

There is a car park right next to the Gardens and free of charge. You are welcome to leave cars over night, but this is at your own risk and the Larmer Tree is not responsible for your cars or belongings.

Accommodation

If you are planning to stay for the weekend, we recommend finding a hotel in Bournemouth and travelling to the Larmer Tree on the day, then taking the coach back.

This will mean you are centrally located and close to us on the Sunday for the beach BBQ (details to follow!). Get in touch if you want advice on where to stay in Bournemouth.
