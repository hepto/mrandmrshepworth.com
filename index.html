The Larmer Tree Gardens

Set in the outstanding landscape of the Cranborne Chase on the border of Dorset & Wiltshire, these beautifully laid out gardens were created by General Pitt Rivers in 1880. Recognised by English Heritage as a Garden of National Importance, they were first privately owned gardens to be opened for public enjoyment.

With 11 acres of gardens, including a large lawn for garden games, home to free roaming peacocks and pair of macaws, and host of a music festival every year, it is the perfect location us to celebrate our wedding with friends and family.
